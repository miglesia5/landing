Bottoms Up Analysis Overview

Bottoms Up Analysis is a methodology that compares quantified workload with current staffing levels. While identifying opportunities for improvement, current team pain points and burdens It requires a detailed analysis of the work items performed. By calculating how long it takes specific work items to be performed, multiplying the number of times that task is performed in a given period and then adding these values together.

The App Concept for Bottoms Up Analysis Web App Intends to calculate and compares task against current staffing levels. The focus of the analysis is to identify potential capacity opened for a work area, over workloads and work distribution. Asides of quantifying potential saving for continual improvement business cases. The Analysis helps our business team take strategic decisions and drive for business areas improvement.
